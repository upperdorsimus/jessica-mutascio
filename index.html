About Jessica

"
"

I never made one of my discoveries through a process of rational thinking.— Albert Einstein

Jessica Mutascio is an award-winning editor whose ability to think beyond the frame has seen her jumping across genres, all while bringing a distinctive edge to commercials, music videos, and both short and longform film.

Comfortable editing everything from action to comedy to fashion, her style and love of collaboration has seen her working with a number of high profile clients and directors across the US and Australia.

When she is not sitting and thinking in dark rooms, Jessica enjoys yoga, running, traveling, and pretending she is on Masterchef. Currently she splits her time between New York City and the San Francisco Bay Area — though she's always down to hop on a plane.
