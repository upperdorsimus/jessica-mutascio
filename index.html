I never made one of my discoveries through a process of rational thinking.— Albert Einstein

Jessica Mutascio is an award-winning editor whose ability to think beyond the frame has seen her jumping across genres, all while bringing a distinctive edge to commercials, music videos, and both short and longform film.

Comfortable editing everything from action to comedy to fashion, her style and love of collaboration has seen her working with high profile clients and directors across the US and Australia.

Currently splitting her time between New York City and the San Francisco Bay Area — though always down to hop on a plane.
